Liquid Glass Element

This page demonstrates a customizable "liquid glass" effect using a Custom Web Component.

Customization

Use the <liquid-glass-element> tag. Customize its appearance by the following attributes:

  • width & height: The dimensions of the element.
  • radius: The corner roundness.
  • shadow-opacity & shadow-blur: Controls the underlying shadow.
  • highlight-opacity & highlight-blur: Controls the top highlight.
  • glass-opacity & glass-blur: Controls the main glass body's transparency and blur.
  • pre-blur & post-blur: Fine-tunes the blur before and after the distortion.
  • chromatic-aberration: Creates a color-fringing effect at the edges.
